MALAYSIA : Eating

Malaysian people talks so loud especially during any festival season or special ocassion. No Sweat! Just blend in!

If you attend traditional Malaysian feast, dont be surprised if you don't see any chairs or tables around you.

There are rules and taboos that you must observe if you happen to be invited to a Malay home for a meal. Malays are also invariably muslims, therefore there is no pork, non-halal meat or alcohol beverages served in a Malay home

We, Malaysian are so proud with our accent and its normal if you heard "lah" at the end of our sentences.

Who gossips to you will gossip of you.
