KOREA : Business Meeting

You have arrived at a business meeting and are going to meet the company's president. You should bow and wait for him to initiate the handshake.

Use both hands if possible when presenting and receiving a business card. If that is not possible, use your right hand and support your right elbow with your left hand.

Always be puntual with your Korean collegues. If you find you are running late, make sure to notify your Korean partner.

Avoids sensative topics during business meeting
